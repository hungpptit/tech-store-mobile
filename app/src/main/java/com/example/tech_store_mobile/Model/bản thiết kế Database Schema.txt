# THIET KE DATABASE SCHEMA - TECH STORE (FIREBASE FIRESTORE)

Tai lieu nay tong hop schema NoSQL cho ung dung Tech Store, phu hop voi cac man hinh:
- Gio hang
- Thanh toan
- Dia chi
- Thong bao
- Tim kiem

## 1) users
**Muc dich:** Luu ho so nguoi dung va cau hinh mac dinh.

**Fields:**
- `userId`: `string` - PK, UID tu Firebase Auth
- `role`: `string` (`user` | `admin`)
- `fullName`: `string`
- `email`: `string`
- `phoneNumber`: `string`
- `dateOfBirth`: `string` (dd/MM/yyyy)
- `gender`: `string` (`Male` | `Female` | `Other`)
- `avatarUrl`: `string` (link Firebase Storage)
- `fcmToken`: `string` (token de gui thong bao chat/push)
- `defaultAddressId`: `string` (tham chieu `addresses.addressId`)
- `defaultPaymentId`: `string` (tham chieu `payment_methods.paymentId`)
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`

## 2) categories
**Muc dich:** Nhom san pham theo danh muc de hien thi va loc.

**Fields:**
- `categoryId`: `string`
- `categoryName`: `string` (vd: Smart Phone, Laptop, Watch, Screen)
- `imageUrl`: `string`
- `displayOrder`: `number` (thu tu hien thi)

## 3) products
**Muc dich:** Luu thong tin san pham va du lieu phuc vu tim kiem.

**Fields:**
- `productId`: `string`
- `categoryId`: `string`
- `productName`: `string`
- `brand`: `string` (vd: Apple, Samsung, MSI, Deli)
- `description`: `string`
- `basePrice`: `number`
- `discountPercentage`: `number`
- `finalPrice`: `number`
- `colors`: `array<string>` (vd: `["#000000", "#FFFFFF"]`)
- `imageUrl`: `string`
- `rating`: `number` (vd: 4.5)
- `reviewCount`: `number`
- `stockQuantity`: `number`
- `isNew`: `boolean`
- `isBestSeller`: `boolean`
- `searchKeywords`: `array<string>` (vd: `["mac", "macbook", "m4"]`)

## 4) addresses
**Muc dich:** Luu danh sach dia chi giao hang cua user.

**Fields:**
- `addressId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string`
- `nickname`: `string` (vd: Home, Office, Parent House)
- `fullAddress`: `string`
- `isDefault`: `boolean`

> Co the trien khai dang sub-collection (`users/{userId}/addresses`) hoac collection rieng.

## 5) payment_methods
**Muc dich:** Luu phuong thuc thanh toan da luu cua user.

**Fields:**
- `paymentId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string`
- `cardType`: `string` (vd: VISA, MasterCard)
- `cardNumber`: `string` (chi luu masked, vd: `**** **** **** 2512`)
- `expiryDate`: `string` (MM/YY)
- `cardHolderName`: `string`
- `isDefault`: `boolean`

## 6) cart
**Muc dich:** Luu trang thai gio hang hien tai cua user (moi user 1 document).

**Fields:**
- `userId`: `string` (co the dung lam document ID)
- `items`: `array<map>`
  - `productId`: `string`
  - `quantity`: `number`
  - `selectedColor`: `string`
  - `priceAtAdded`: `number`
- `updatedAt`: `timestamp`

## 7) orders
**Muc dich:** Luu don hang, lich su xu ly va thong tin snapshot luc mua.

**Fields:**
- `orderId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string`
- `orderDate`: `timestamp`
- `status`: `string` (`Packing` | `Picked` | `In Transit` | `Delivered` | `Completed`)
- `items`: `array<map>`
  - `productId`: `string`
  - `productName`: `string`
  - `quantity`: `number`
  - `price`: `number`
  - `imageUrl`: `string`
  - `color`: `string`
- `summary`: `map`
  - `subTotal`: `number`
  - `shippingFee`: `number`
  - `vatAmount`: `number`
  - `totalAmount`: `number`
- `shippingAddress`: `map` (snapshot dia chi khi dat hang)
- `paymentMethod`: `string` (vd: Visa 2512, Cash)
- `trackingHistory`: `array<map>`
  - `statusName`: `string`
  - `location`: `string`
  - `timestamp`: `timestamp`

## 8) hoa_dons
**Muc dich:** Luu hoa don thanh toan/hoa don dien tu duoc tao tu don hang, phuc vu doi soat va lich su thanh toan.

**Fields:**
- `hoaDonId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string` - UID tu Firebase Auth
- `orderId`: `string` - lien ket den `orders.orderId`
- `invoiceNumber`: `string` - ma hoa don hien thi cho nguoi dung
- `issueDate`: `timestamp`
- `dueDate`: `timestamp` (neu co han thanh toan)
- `paymentStatus`: `string` (`Pending` | `Paid` | `Failed` | `Refunded`)
- `paymentMethod`: `string` (vd: Visa, Cash)
- `paymentGateway`: `string` (vd: Stripe)
- `transactionId`: `string` (ma giao dich tu cong thanh toan)
- `items`: `array<map>`
  - `productId`: `string`
  - `productName`: `string`
  - `quantity`: `number`
  - `price`: `number`
  - `imageUrl`: `string`
  - `color`: `string`
- `summary`: `map`
  - `subTotal`: `number`
  - `shippingFee`: `number`
  - `vatAmount`: `number`
  - `discountAmount`: `number`
  - `totalAmount`: `number`
- `shippingAddress`: `map` (snapshot dia chi luc thanh toan)
- `note`: `string`
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`

## 9) lich_su_thanh_toans
**Muc dich:** Luu tung giao dich thanh toan de truy vet trang thai, doi soat va hien thi lich su cho user.

**Fields:**
- `paymentHistoryId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string` - UID tu Firebase Auth
- `hoaDonId`: `string` - lien ket den `hoa_dons.hoaDonId`
- `orderId`: `string` - lien ket den `orders.orderId`
- `paymentMethod`: `string` (vd: Visa, Cash)
- `paymentGateway`: `string` (vd: Stripe)
- `paymentStatus`: `string` (`Pending` | `Succeeded` | `Failed` | `Canceled` | `Refunded`)
- `transactionId`: `string`
- `stripePaymentIntentId`: `string`
- `amount`: `number`
- `currency`: `string` (vd: USD)
- `paidAt`: `timestamp`
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`
- `failureReason`: `string`
- `note`: `string`

## 10) notifications (Personal & Target)
**Muc dich:** Luu thong bao ca nhan (vd: trang thai don hang, nhac nho thanh toan). Moi document la 1 thong bao cho 1 user duy nhat.

**Fields:**
- `notificationId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string` - UID nguoi nhan
- `orderId`: `string` - (Optional) lien ket den don hang de bam vao xem chi tiet
- `title`: `string`
- `content`: `string`
- `type`: `string` (`Order` | `System` | `Personal`)
- `isRead`: `boolean`
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`

## 10.1) global_announcements (Admin Broadcast)
**Muc dich:** Admin soan 1 lan va gui cho toan bo nguoi dung (Khuyen mai, bao tri). User Android se doc song song bang nay va bang notifications.

**Fields:**
- `announcementId`: `string`
- `adminId`: `string` - UID cua Admin tao
- `title`: `string`
- `content`: `string`
- `imageUrl`: `string` - (Optional) anh banner khuyen mai
- `type`: `string` (`Promotion` | `System`) - (Optional) loai thong bao
- `targetGroup`: `string` (`All` | `Member` | `VIP`) - (Optional) nhom nguoi nhan
- `scheduledAt`: `timestamp` - thoi gian hen gio gui
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`

## 11) reviews
**Muc dich:** Luu danh gia san pham cua nguoi dung.

**Fields:**
- `reviewId`: `string`
- `productId`: `string`
- `userId`: `string`
- `userName`: `string` (snapshot de hien thi nhanh)
- `rating`: `number`
- `comment`: `string`
- `createdAt`: `timestamp`

## 12) search_history
**Muc dich:** Luu tu khoa tim kiem gan day cua user.

**Fields:**
- `userId`: `string` (document ID)
- `recentKeywords`: `array<string>` (toi da 5-10 tu khoa)

## 13) rooms
**Muc dich:** Luu danh sach cuoc hoi thoai chat giua user Android va admin Web.

**Cach dung:**
- Document ID nen dat theo `userId` de admin tim nhanh cuoc hoi thoai cua tung user.
- Duong dan co the dung: `rooms/{userId}`

**Fields:**
- `userId`: `string` - UID cua khach hang
- `userName`: `string` - ten hien thi cua khach hang
- `lastMessage`: `string` - noi dung tin nhan cuoi cung
- `updatedAt`: `timestamp` - de sap xep phong chat moi nhat len dau
- `userUnreadCount`: `number` - so tin nhan admin gui ma user chua doc
- `adminUnreadCount`: `number` - so tin nhan user gui ma admin chua doc
- `isStarredByAdmin`: `boolean` - Đánh dấu phòng chat quan trọng (Gắn sao)
- `isSpamByAdmin`: `boolean` - Đánh dấu spam
- `isDeletedByAdmin`: `boolean` - Đánh dấu xóa
- `isImportByAdmin`: `boolean` - Đánh dấu quan trọng

## 14) messages
**Muc dich:** Luu danh sach tin nhan ben trong tung room chat.

**Cach dung:**
- Day la sub-collection cua `rooms`
- Duong dan: `rooms/{userId}/messages/{messageId}`
- `messageId` nen de Firebase tu sinh (Auto-ID)

**Fields:**
- `senderId`: `string` - UID nguoi gui
- `receiverId`: `string` - UID nguoi nhan
- `content`: `string` - noi dung text
- `type`: `string` (`text` | `image` | `invoice`)
- `fileUrl`: `string` - link anh/file neu co
- `createdAt`: `timestamp` - bat buoc dung `ServerTimestamp`

> Ghi chu: voi web admin, co the doc danh sach room tu `rooms`, sau do vao `messages` cua tung room de xem noi dung chat.

## Cong dung tung model (Java)

- `User`: Dai dien ho so tai khoan, su dung o man hinh profile, cai dat, dia chi va thanh toan mac dinh.
- `Category`: Nguon du lieu cho menu danh muc, bo loc homepage/search.
- `Product`: Model trung tam cua catalog; dung cho danh sach san pham, chi tiet san pham, tim kiem.
- `Address`: Quan ly dia chi giao hang cua user; dung trong checkout va profile.
- `PaymentMethod`: Quan ly thong tin thanh toan da luu (masked card); dung trong checkout.
- `Cart`: Trang thai gio hang theo user (tong quan gio + danh sach item + thoi gian cap nhat).
- `CartItem`: Tung dong san pham trong gio (so luong, mau, gia tai thoi diem them).
- `Order`: Don hang tong the, gom trang thai, danh sach item, thanh toan, dia chi, tracking.
- `HoaDon`: Hoa don thanh toan, snapshot thong tin don hang va trang thai thanh toan de doi soat.
- `LichSuThanhToan`: Nhat ky giao dich thanh toan theo user/hoa don, phuc vu tra cuu va retry.
- `OrderItem`: Snapshot san pham trong don hang de tranh sai lech khi gia/thong tin san pham thay doi sau nay.
- `OrderSummary`: Tong hop tien truoc/sau phi ship va VAT de render man hinh thanh toan/chi tiet don.
- `ShippingAddressSnapshot`: Snapshot dia chi giao nhan tai thoi diem dat don.
- `TrackingHistoryItem`: Tung moc trang thai van chuyen/de xu ly cua don hang.
- `UserNotification`: Model thong bao theo user; dung cho inbox thong bao va badge chua doc.
- `Review`: Noi dung danh gia/sao theo san pham; dung de tinh rating trung binh va hien thi review.
- `SearchHistory`: Luu lich su tim kiem gan day de de xuat nhanh tren man hinh search.

## Luu y trien khai Firebase

1. **Security Rules**
   - Chi cho phep user doc/ghi du lieu cua chinh ho (dua vao `request.auth.uid == userId`).

2. **Tim kiem san pham**
   - Dung `searchKeywords` + truy van:
   - `where("searchKeywords", "array-contains", query)`

3. **Gia tien**
   - Luon luu dang `number` de tinh toan.
   - VAT nen dat thanh hang so trong app de de bao tri.
